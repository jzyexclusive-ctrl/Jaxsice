Welcome to D'Roadz

Nestled along the bustling NH-44 at Nanguneri Toll Plaza, D'Roadz Restaurant has become a beloved sanctuary for travelers and families seeking more than just a meal—we offer an experience.

Since our inception, we've been dedicated to serving authentic Indian flavors with a modern touch, combining traditional recipes with contemporary culinary techniques. Our spacious, family-friendly ambiance and commitment to quality have made us the preferred dining destination for highway travelers.

Whether you're stopping for a quick refreshment or settling in for a leisurely family meal, D'Roadz promises comfort, quality, and unforgettable flavors at every visit.
